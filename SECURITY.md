# Security policy

Revu reads your local League of Legends client data and your Riot Match
history through a thin Cloudflare Worker proxy; everything else lives on
your machine. If you find a security issue — credential exposure, an
unsafe file write, anything that could affect another user's data — please
**don't open a public GitHub issue.** Email the maintainer at
[samifawcett.nyc@gmail.com](mailto:samifawcett.nyc@gmail.com) with
"Revu security" in the subject line, and I'll respond within 48 hours
on weekdays.

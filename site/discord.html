Pre-alpha feedback, bugs, and release help

Revu is still early. The Discord is the best place to send rough feedback, ask for setup help, report update issues, and talk through what should change before the app reaches a wider alpha.

Good feedback includes what you were trying to do, what felt confusing, screenshots when useful, and the Revu version from the app footer.
